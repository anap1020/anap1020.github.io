The Royal Peach Palette

This web is basically about make up, to be more specific about the royal peach palette from Kylie Cosmetics, The #KylieCosmetics Kyshadow pressed powder eye shadow palette is your secret weapon to create the perfect Kylie eye. Each Royal Peach Palette comes with 12 pressed powder eye shadows that can be used together to recreate Kylie’s favorite looks or customize your own. The Kyshadow kit pressed powder eye shadows are talc and paraben free, have medium buildable coverage and a velvety smooth texture. Why I Love this palette!

I fell In love since the first moment I saw the palette on Instagram in the official account @kyliecosmetics because the package looks really cool and minimalist also the colors where awesome and it has a good brush. I was looking for that shades because they are in trend and it matches with my color of eyes. I have to say that I also like Kylie´s work so It was something perfect, the formula is really good and the shades are pigmented, specially peachy and mojito (my favs) Peachy is a bold, medium-dark orange with a hint of red, which mutes the color a bit, paired with a matte finish. The eyeshadow felt dusty in the pan, but it did not result in noticeable fallout when I was applying it to the lid, as the color adhered well to bare skin. It had good color payoff with a soft, blendable consistency. Mojito is a bright, golden chartreuse green with a metallic finish. It leaned more golden than it did green. It had rich, opaque color coverage, this palette it´s really easy to use and finally it's not really expensive. I will add a video of a tutorial with other photos.
